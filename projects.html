Designed with a clean, intuitive interface, it presents all your tasks in a single, streamlined view to help you stay organized and focused on your deadlines. With no unnecessary features or distractions, Do or Done keeps you centered on what truly matters — getting things done.

QuackCare - Mental Wellness App

QuackCare is a mental wellness app designed to support users on their emotional well-being journey. Its primary feature is an AI-powered chatbot that listens, provides compassionate guidance, and can detect and report signs of extreme behavior or distress. Beyond the chatbot, QuackCare offers a range of supportive tools, including personalized affirmations, guided breathing exercises, and journaling prompts, as well as access to practical resources like nearby mental health professionals and national helpline numbers. Powered by Ollama and ChatGPT APIs, QuackCare blends advanced AI with evidence-based practices to promote a healthier mind.
